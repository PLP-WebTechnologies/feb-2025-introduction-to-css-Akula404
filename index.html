Today we'll be discussing about Oceans

Oceans cover more than 70% of the Earth's surface and play a crucial role in sustaining life on our planet. They are home to a vast diversity of marine species, from microscopic plankton to massive whales, forming intricate ecosystems that support global biodiversity


Importance of Oceans

Oceans are vital for regulating the Earth's climate, absorbing carbon dioxide, and providing oxygen. They also support global economies through fishing, tourism, and shipping industries.
